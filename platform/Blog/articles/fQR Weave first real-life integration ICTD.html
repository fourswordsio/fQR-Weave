fQR Weave first real-life integration: ICTD



By today, fQR Weave - the arweave based app - have reached an agreement with ICTD, an academic center in Lebanon. With this collaboration, ICTD is going to be the first academic center in Lebanon, but even in the middle-east using blockchain technology in certificate's authentication.

What is ICTD ?

According to ICTD (International Center For Training & Developments) website:

"We are a team of academics and technicians specializing in consulting, training and quality management. We have seen an urgent need for training and development programs that will enhance the quality of training and education in various disciplines, by ensuring international standards of professionalism and reference"

ICTD Statistics:

  • 461 Exams

  • 6784 Students

  • 164 Courses

  • 153 Trainers

How ICTD Will Use fQR Weave ?

ICTD will use fQR Weave service to authenticate their students certificates on arweave blockchain (permaweb). With this integration, each certificate will have a fQR on it containing the following metadata:

  • Student's name

  • Student's Birthday

  • Course Name

  • Course Price (tariff)

  • Certificate Issue Date


As planned before, we have created a custom "fQR Generator" instance for ICTD, to generate their own pre-decided data-type and informations. And for sure, only ICTD verified address is able to generate fQRs which are scanned and decrypted by fQR Reader.


How Can ICTD Take fQR Weave to a higher level ?

Beside the integration of fQRs in the ICTD intern-area, this academic center - ICTD - leads, organise, and sponsor the National Robotics Competition (NRC) in Lebanon, side by side with the lebanese ministry of education. ICTD is responsible to issue and distribute the participants/winners certificates, which will have a fQR on it!
