Customer's tool: fQR Reader

fQR Reader is the fQR scanner which is used to read the fQR code and extract transaction's data. In its first release, the user have to search for tx data "manually" after scanning the fQR code. This is made in this way to make new users more friendly with blockchain as technology, and Arweave aswell.

In addition to the friendly manner, thesearch functionality is implemented to make the fQR Reader like an explorer too.

How To Identify Verified Transactions (product's owner)

The scanner is used to read fQR codes and differentiate between verified and unverfied products. At the moment that a customer scan the fQR code, he will get the transaction data ID with a legitimate tag: verified or unverified . If the fQR was generated from a verified sellers, the customer can search for the ID data.

About Users Privacy And Data Mining:

Companies collecting data for pubs and restaurants to help them fulfil their contact-tracing duties are harvesting confidential customer information to sell.

Legal experts have warned of a “privacy crisis” caused by a rise in companies exploiting QR barcodes to take names, addresses, telephone numbers and email details, before passing them on to marketers, credit companies and insurance brokers.

But those concerns don't exist with fQR codes, because the platform's source code is open source and available for anyone. Also, fQR Reader (& Generator for product's owner) will be published in arweave permaweb, hence it will be immutable and safe from any modification or exploit.
